Our Story

Why we want to implement this website?

During the process of reading the news, members of our team discovered the social problems that are frequently caused by car accidents in Victoria, Australia, and found that car accidents kill many people every year. Because of this problem, we fell into contemplation and thought of wanting tacitly. Explore and help reduce the occurrence of this phenomenon, thus, the birth of this website.

What is the goal of our website?

In this website, we can help select a route according to the required itinerary and display historical accidents in the route to remind users. In addition, users can also select the appropriate departure time and date and observe the weather through our website. Of course, for some people who want to understand the accident.
