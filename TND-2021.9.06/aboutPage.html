What is Trust & Drive?

In recent years, most accidents on Victorian roads have occurred because drivers are unfamiliar with the overall environment of driving on the road. The purpose of Trust & Drive is to help people who are new to the driving environment of Victorian roads to prepare for safe driving and avoid accidents.

Opened data source

The primary data set provided by VicRoads, which is reliable, and the source is open and transparent.

Human Centered Design

Our website complies with Australian privacy principles and will not steal, use or disclose personal information.

Reliable data analysis

The data analysis provided by Trust N Drive is composed of a professional data science team.

Resources

Victoria has some of the most magnificent roads globally, such as the Great Ocean Road and Dandenong Mountain. Relying on an extensive and well-maintained road network, driving is one of Victoria's most common and convenient transportation modes. Left-hand driving rules are implemented throughout Australia, and the police strictly enforce traffic laws to ensure that you and your family can reach your destination safely. You can go to the official website to find out what you need to know about roads and driving laws in Victoria.
