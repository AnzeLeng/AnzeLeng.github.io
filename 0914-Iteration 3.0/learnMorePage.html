What are the top 5 causes of road accidents?

Top causes for Accidents in Australia:

#1. Speeding: Speeding accounts for one of the major causes of accidents in Australia.
#2. Distraction: navigation, cell phones, and sound, which increases the number of accidents caused by people being distracted.
#3. Drive after drunk: Approximately 30% of the total accidents were alcohol related.
#4. Fatigue: Tired driving will hinder the driver's reaction ability and decision-making level, increasing the probability of accidents.
#5. Young Drivers: Drivers who are not eligible account for the majority of accidents.

When do road accidents happen frequently? Day or night?

Perhaps to the surprise of many people, there are far more traffic accidents during the day than at night. Statistics show that more than half of accidents occur during the day, and only one-third of accidents occur at night.
